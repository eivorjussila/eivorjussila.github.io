⛣ who are we?

we are a small start up, based in our cosy village by the sea in Haiphong, Vietnam. currently we have 25 members who are actively working because we believe in making a healthy space for everyone to grow and our mission is to give our hometown a more lively and modern touch. ❧

⛣ our purpose

we provide solution for basic needs of human being, such as: hygene, water, food, clothing, and sex education. we believe in preserving the vibrance of a small town culture, but at the same time, want to give it a modern touch of wizardry magic of technology. ❧
